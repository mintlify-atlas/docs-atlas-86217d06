Before you commit to setting up your AI customer support agent, it helps to see exactly what you're getting. My AskAI offers a complete product demonstration — a guided, end-to-end walkthrough of the platform that shows how every major feature works together in a real business context. Whether you're evaluating the platform for the first time or onboarding stakeholders who need to see it in action before they sign off, the demo gives you a clear, honest picture of what My AskAI can do.

## Watch the full product demo

The recorded product demo covers the complete platform — from the moment you sign up to a live agent handling customer queries and surfacing insights. You can watch it on demand at [myaskai.com](https://myaskai.com), where you'll find the latest version of the walkthrough alongside the most up-to-date feature coverage.

<Note>
  You can start a free 30-day trial directly at [myaskai.com](https://myaskai.com) with no sales call or demo required. Trial accounts have access to all platform features from day one.
</Note>
