My AskAI supports multiple ways to sign in — magic link (passwordless email), Google sign-in (via your work Google account), and password login. This page covers everything related to managing your personal account access, from setting up a password to closing your account entirely.

## Creating Your Account

Sign up at [myaskai.com](https://myaskai.com) using your email address. By default, My AskAI uses **magic link login** — you enter your email and receive a one-time login link in your inbox. No password is required unless you choose to set one.

<Note>
  Invited team members **cannot** sign in with Google. They must use magic-link (email) login when accepting an invitation.
</Note>

## Setting a Password

If you'd prefer to log in with a password rather than a magic link, you can set one up at any time:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Login Page">
    Navigate to [myaskai.com](https://myaskai.com) and scroll to the bottom of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Password Login'">
    Select the **Password Login** option at the bottom of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Type in the email address associated with your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request a Password">
    Click **"Send me a new password"**. My AskAI will email you a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In">
    Copy the password from the email, paste it into the password field, and click **Login**. You can change this password after signing in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Setting up password login is especially useful if you want to share dashboard access with colleagues, or if magic-link emails are being delayed or filtered by your email provider.
</Tip>

## Resetting a Forgotten Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Password Reset Page">
    Navigate to [myaskai.com/login_password](https://myaskai.com/login_password). You can also find this link at the bottom of the My AskAI landing page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use 'Send Me a New Password'">
    Enter your email address and click **"Send me a new password"**. A new password will be emailed to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In with the New Password">
    Use the password from the email to log in. You can update it from your account settings once you're in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA / MFA)

<Accordion title="Does My AskAI have built-in 2FA or MFA?">
  My AskAI does not currently have native 2FA or MFA built into the login flow.

  However, you can achieve equivalent security by signing up with a **work Google Workspace account** (not a personal Gmail address). Google Workspace supports enforced 2FA/MFA at the organisation level, and that protection carries through to your My AskAI login via Google sign-in.

  If your organisation requires native 2FA/MFA support, get in touch via the support chat and ask about **Enterprise plan** options.
</Accordion>

## Single Sign-On (SSO)

My AskAI supports **Google sign-in** using a work Google Workspace account. This allows teams already using Google for identity management to sign in without a separate password.

<Info>
  Wider SSO/SAML support for other identity providers (Okta, Azure AD, etc.) is available on Enterprise plans. Contact the team via chat to discuss your requirements.
</Info>

## Changing Your Email Address

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account Settings">
    In your My AskAI dashboard, click **Account Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Admin Email">
    Find the **Admin email** field, enter your new email address, and click **"Update"**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Make sure the new email address is one you can access before saving. Your new email will become the login address for your account.
</Warning>

## Closing Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account Settings">
    Click **Account Settings** in your My AskAI dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to the Bottom">
    Scroll all the way to the bottom of the Account Settings page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Delete my account and all my data'">
    Click the **"Delete my account and all my data"** button. Account deletion can take up to 24 hours to process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**. All your data, agents, content, and conversation history will be removed. Make sure to download any data you need before proceeding.

  My AskAI is not affiliated with the [Ask AI](https://askaichat.app/) mobile app. If you are trying to close an account with that product, please contact them directly.
</Warning>
