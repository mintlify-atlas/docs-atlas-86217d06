My AskAI offers a range of subscription plans designed to scale with your business — from early-stage startups handling a few hundred conversations a month to enterprise teams managing tens of thousands of tickets across multiple brands. Every new account starts with a **30-day free trial** (no credit card required), so you can explore the full feature set before committing to a paid plan.

<Note>
  For the most up-to-date plan pricing, feature lists, and limits, visit [myaskai.com/pricing](https://myaskai.com/pricing). Prices are not published here to ensure you always see the latest information.
</Note>

## What to Look for When Choosing a Plan

Before selecting a plan, consider the following key dimensions:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Conversation Limits" icon="message-lines">
    Each plan includes a monthly conversation allowance. Additional conversations beyond your limit are charged at a per-conversation rate via **Flex pricing** — your agent never stops responding mid-month.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Channel Integrations" icon="plug">
    Connect My AskAI to your existing helpdesk (Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk, Gorgias, HubSpot, and more). Higher plans may unlock additional or priority channel access.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Seats" icon="users">
    Invite colleagues to view insights, manage content, and configure your agent. Pro and Enterprise plans include unlimited team seats.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Advanced Features" icon="star">
    Features like **Multibrand** (multiple agents for separate brands), **AI Actions** (connecting the agent to internal systems), and **Remove Branding** are available as add-ons or on higher-tier plans.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What Counts as a Conversation?

Because My AskAI works across many different channels, conversation counting varies slightly by integration:

| Integration type | How a conversation is counted |
|---|---|
| My AskAI widget | Any number of AI responses in a single chat thread within a 1-hour session |
| Live chat integrations (e.g. Zendesk Messaging, Intercom, Freshchat) | Every **2 AI responses** = 1 conversation credit |
| Ticket/email integrations (e.g. Zendesk Tickets, Freshdesk, Gorgias email) | First AI reply = 1 credit; each subsequent reply = 0.5 credits |

<Tip>
  After connecting your helpdesk and running your trial for 7 days, a **cost estimator** will appear in **Account Settings → Billing** showing your projected monthly cost based on actual usage.
</Tip>

## Free Trial

When you sign up for My AskAI you automatically begin a **30-day free trial** with full access to paid plan features — no credit card required. Trials can be extended on a case-by-case basis; contact the team via chat if you need more time.

<Info>
  My AskAI does **not** offer lifetime subscriptions. If you've seen a lifetime deal under a similar name, that is a separate product unaffiliated with My AskAI.
</Info>

## Upgrading or Downgrading Your Plan

Changing plans is done directly through the Stripe billing portal:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account Settings">
    Go to **Account Settings** in your My AskAI dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Billing">
    Click on **Billing** in the left-hand menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Stripe">
    Click **"Manage subscription on Stripe"** to open the Stripe customer portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your New Plan">
    From the Stripe portal, choose your desired plan. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Usage-Based (Flex) Costs

All paid plans include Flex pricing — your AI agent **continues responding** even after you exceed your monthly conversation limit. Overage charges are added to the following month's invoice.

- Pro plan: **$0.12** per conversation over the limit
- Scale/Enterprise plans: **$0.10** per conversation over the limit

You can monitor your current usage at any time in **Account Settings → Billing**.

<Warning>
  Flex pricing cannot be disabled. If you're concerned about runaway costs, monitor your usage dashboard regularly and consider upgrading to a higher-limit plan.
</Warning>

## Discount Programs

My AskAI offers **20% off all plans** (excluding Enterprise) for registered charities, non-profits, and NGOs. To claim the discount, share proof of your organisation's status via the support chat — a link to your website or charity register listing is sufficient.

No student plans are currently available.
