My AskAI is built to be flexible — not just for businesses deploying their own AI support agent, but also for agencies, consultants, and developers who want to build and sell AI-powered support solutions on behalf of their clients. This page covers the options available for reselling and whitelabelling My AskAI.

<Note>
  My AskAI no longer operates an affiliate referral programme. The options below focus on reselling and whitelabelling for commercial use.
</Note>

## Reselling vs Whitelabelling at a Glance

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Reselling" icon="store">
    Create AI agents for your clients using your own My AskAI account. You control the setup, configure the agent, connect their content, and install it on their site. You set your own pricing — My AskAI doesn't dictate what you charge your clients.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Whitelabelling" icon="paintbrush">
    Remove My AskAI branding from the chat widget and present the product entirely under your own brand or your client's brand. Combine with custom styling to deliver a fully branded experience.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Reselling My AskAI

You can resell My AskAI to clients freely. There is no formal reseller application required — simply create AI agents on behalf of your clients within your account and charge them whatever price you choose.

**Common reseller model:**
1. Sign up for a My AskAI plan that covers all your client agents
2. Create a separate AI agent for each client, trained on their content
3. Install the widget (or integrate with their helpdesk) on their website or support tool
4. Bill your clients directly at your own rates

<Tip>
  Use the [Multibrand](/features/multibrand) feature to manage multiple client agents from a single My AskAI account. Each agent has its own knowledge base, customisation settings, and channel integrations.
</Tip>

## Whitelabelling Options

There are three ways to deliver a whitelabelled My AskAI experience:

<Accordion title="Option 1: Remove Branding Add-on">
  The simplest whitelabel approach. Add the **Remove Branding** add-on ($49/month) to remove My AskAI's logo and branding from the chat widget. Combine this with the full [customisation](/features/customization) options — custom colours, fonts, avatar, and widget text — to present an agent that looks entirely your own.

  This works well for agencies installing agents on client websites.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Option 2: Build via the My AskAI API">
  For developers and technical agencies, you can consume My AskAI entirely through the API — creating agents, adding content, and handling conversations programmatically. This allows you to build fully custom front-end experiences with no visible My AskAI branding at all.

  See the [API documentation](/api/introduction) for details on the available endpoints.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Option 3: Request a White-labeled Instance">
  For larger agencies or enterprise partners who want a fully separate, branded deployment of My AskAI — including separate dashboards per client, custom domain, and billing passthrough — My AskAI can build a white-labeled copy for use within your organisation.

  Contact the team via chat to discuss this option and your requirements.
</Accordion>

## What's Included in a Whitelabel Setup

Depending on the approach you choose, a whitelabel arrangement can include:

- ✅ My AskAI branding removed from the chat widget
- ✅ Custom colours, logo, fonts, and avatar via the Customisation settings
- ✅ Separate knowledge bases per client (via Multibrand)
- ✅ Your own pricing and billing to end clients
- ✅ API access for fully custom front-end builds
- ✅ Separate dashboards per client (custom instance — contact team)

## How to Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Up Your My AskAI Account">
    Sign up at [myaskai.com](https://myaskai.com) and choose a plan that suits the number of client agents you plan to manage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Client Agents">
    Use the **Multibrand** feature to create and manage separate agents for each client, each with their own knowledge base and settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove Branding (Optional)">
    Add the **Remove Branding** add-on via **Account Settings → Billing → "Manage subscription on Stripe"** to remove My AskAI branding from your client-facing widgets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customise Each Agent">
    Use the **Customisation** settings for each agent to match the look and feel of your client's brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact My AskAI for a Full Whitelabel">
    If you need a fully separate branded instance (custom dashboards, custom domain, billing passthrough), reach out to the My AskAI team via chat to discuss the custom whitelabel programme.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  For questions about custom whitelabel builds, enterprise reseller arrangements, or API-based integrations at scale, contact the My AskAI team directly via the chat widget on [myaskai.com](https://myaskai.com).
</Info>
