My AskAI is an AI-powered customer support agent platform built for businesses that want to handle support at scale — without scaling headcount. Founded in early 2023, My AskAI was born from a real problem: as the team's user base grew, managing customer support manually meant valuable feedback, bug reports, and feature requests were getting lost in conversations. The solution was an AI agent that doesn't just answer questions — it listens, learns, and distills every conversation into actionable intelligence your product team can actually use.

At its core, My AskAI trains an AI agent on your own content (help docs, website, knowledge base, historic tickets) and deploys it as the first line of defence for your customer support. The agent resolves up to 75% of incoming questions automatically, routes the rest to human agents seamlessly, and generates a continuous stream of insights — categorised feedback, identified bugs, surfaced feature requests — so your team can focus on improving the product rather than fielding repetitive queries.
