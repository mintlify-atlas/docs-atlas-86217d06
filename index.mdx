My AskAI is an AI customer support agent platform that lets you deploy a fully trained support bot in minutes — no ML expertise required. It learns from your existing documentation, help center articles, and past tickets, then automatically handles routine support questions while routing complex issues to your human team.

## Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your AI agent">
    Sign up at [myaskai.com](https://myaskai.com) and create your first AI customer support agent. Give it a name and connect your knowledge sources — website URLs, help articles, or uploaded documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train on your content">
    My AskAI indexes your content automatically. Add your website URL, paste in your help center link, or upload PDFs and documents. The agent learns from everything you provide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test before you go live">
    Use the built-in testing environment to ask questions and verify your agent responds accurately. Adjust knowledge sources, custom answers, and guidance before publishing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy to your channel">
    Embed the chat widget on your website with a single script tag, or connect to Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk, HubSpot, Slack, or Microsoft Teams.
  </Step>
</Steps>
