## How to Connect Your Shopify Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the My AskAI app from the Shopify App Store">
    Find and install the [My AskAI AI Support Agent app](https://apps.shopify.com/my-askai-ai-support-agent) from the Shopify App Store. No developer is required — this is a standard OAuth app install.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect from your My AskAI Dashboard">
    Log in to your [Dashboard](https://myaskai.com/dashboard) and go to **Knowledge > Content > Connect Shopify store**, then click **Connect Shopify store**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your store name">
    Enter your Shopify store name (the subdomain from your admin URL — for example, if your admin URL is `yourstore.myshopify.com`, your store name is `yourstore`).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    My AskAI will automatically sync your:
    - **Pages** — all published store pages and blog posts
    - **Products** — full product catalogue including descriptions, variants, and pricing
    - **Customer data** — order history, addresses, and account details for verified customers

    You'll see pages and products appear in your content list almost immediately. Customer data becomes available for live order lookups once the sync is complete.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and manage your synced content">
    Click **Content (x pages/products)** to see every item your agent can access. If you want to hide specific pages from the agent's knowledge (for example, a staging page or internal document), toggle the **Public** switch next to that item to **Private**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Embed the chat widget on your Shopify store">
    Go to **Channels** in your Dashboard and copy the widget embed code. Paste it into your Shopify theme's `theme.liquid` file, or use the My AskAI app within your Shopify admin to add it without editing code. The chat bubble will then appear on every page of your store.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Order Status Checks Work

When a customer asks about their order, the agent uses a **Task and Tool** that My AskAI automatically creates for you during Shopify setup. The flow is:

1. The agent detects the user is asking about an order
2. If the user is unverified (e.g. using the website chat widget), the task collects their **email address** and **order ID**
3. The tool calls Shopify's API with those details
4. The agent responds with the order's status, items, and tracking link

**Example conversation on your website widget:**

> *Customer:* "Where is my order?"
> *Agent:* "I'd be happy to help! Could you please share your order number and the email address you used to place the order?"
> *Customer:* "Order #4521, sarah@example.com"
> *Agent:* "Thanks Sarah! Your order #4521 (Organic Cotton Tote Bag, Navy) was dispatched on 4 November and is currently in transit with DPD. You can track it here: [track your order](https://track.dpd.co.uk/xyz123)."

If the customer is contacting you through a helpdesk integration like Gorgias or Zendesk, their email is already verified, so the agent can look up their orders without asking.

## Out-of-the-Box AI Actions for Shopify

When you connect Shopify, My AskAI automatically creates the following tools and tasks in your **Tasks & Tools** section:

| Tool / Task | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Get order status** | Retrieves order details by email and order ID from Shopify |
| **Order status task** | Multi-turn task that collects email and order ID from unverified users, then returns full order information |

You can view and edit both from **Tasks & Tools** in your Dashboard — for example, to add custom logic, change the tone, or extend the flow to include returns handling.

## Managing Customer Data Sync

By default, customer order data is synced and available to your agent. If you need to turn this off:

1. Go to **Knowledge > Content > Connected Shopify store**
2. Click the **⚙️ Settings icon**
3. Expand **Configure your Shopify connection**
4. Toggle **AI agent access to customer data** to **Off**

With this off, the agent will still be able to answer product and policy questions but will not be able to look up individual orders or customer records.

## Shopify Markets and Multi-Store Support

My AskAI works with Shopify Markets out of the box:

- **Multi-language:** The agent auto-detects the customer's language and responds accordingly — no configuration needed. 95+ languages supported.
- **Multi-currency:** Order details including prices are returned in the currency the customer used to purchase.
- **Multiple storefronts:** If you operate separate storefronts for different regions, you can add the My AskAI widget to each one using the same embed code.

## Upgrading Your Plan via Shopify

If you subscribed to My AskAI through the Shopify App Store, you must manage your plan upgrade from within the Shopify admin. To switch from a monthly to an annual plan, contact the My AskAI team by typing "Talk to a person" in the support chat widget.

## Disconnecting Shopify

To disconnect your Shopify store and remove all synced content and customer data, uninstall the My AskAI app from your Shopify admin:

**Settings (⚙️) > Apps and sales channels > My AskAI > ⋯ > Uninstall**

Your account and all associated data will be deleted within 48 hours of uninstalling.
